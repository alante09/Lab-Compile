Haikyuu!

Haikyuu! is about a young boy named Shoyo Hinata, who has a talent for jumping really high despite his small height. He became obsessed with volleyball after catching a glimpse of Karasuno High School playing in Nationals on TV. Of short stature himself, Hinata is inspired by a player the commentators nickname 'The Little Giant', Karasuno's short but talented wing spiker.
